About Us

Our Mission

xoldr was created with a simple goal: to provide everyone with a free, secure, and easy-to-use password generator. In an age where cyber threats are constantly evolving, strong passwords are your first line of defense against hackers and data breaches.

What We Do

We offer a powerful password generation tool that creates cryptographically secure passwords right in your browser. Our generator uses the Web Crypto API to ensure true randomness, making your passwords virtually impossible to guess or crack.

100% Privacy Focused

Your passwords never leave your device. All generation happens locally in your browser — we never see, store, or transmit any passwords you create.

Why Choose xoldr?

  • Secure: Uses cryptographic randomness for truly unpredictable passwords
  • Private: Everything runs locally — no data is sent to servers
  • Free: No signup, no fees, no limitations
  • Simple: Clean interface that anyone can use
  • Customizable: Control length and character types to meet any requirements

Our Commitment

We believe that online security should be accessible to everyone. That's why xoldr is completely free to use, with no hidden costs or premium features locked behind paywalls. We're committed to maintaining a fast, reliable, and ad-supported service that puts your security first.
